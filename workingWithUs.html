MANUFACTURE

Manufacturing of any piece of bespoke furniture is done with experience combined with skill and attention to detail. We always use high quality materials and fittings and continually improving and updating machinery and tooling department. We always try to complete as much of the project as possible before installation allowing us to minimise the installation time.

INSTALLATION

We have been with the business for many years and are experts in fitting and finishing bespoke furniture. Our aim is to develop a long term relationship with our clients. Therefore, whether it is working on a current project or coming back years later to attend to a request our ongoing customer care is very important to us.

CONSULTATION

During your first contact with Valmark Carpentry we will discuss the details about the intended project. At this stage we will get general understanding of your requiments and get everything prepared for our first meeting. It normally takes place in your property. Then we listen to your ideas, look at the concerned area, take measurements, discuss functionality and materials. Many clients produce basic sketches which can be useful reference point in the design.

DESIGN

The next stage in the project is designing the furniture. From the taken measurements and information we produce scaled three dimensional sketch of the room and the product required. Normally, then we return to you and present our ideas, drawings produced for the current project and examples of photos of similar completed projects. When agreement with the final design is reached we can start with manufacturing and installation.
